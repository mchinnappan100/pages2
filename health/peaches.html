Top health benefits

Rich in antioxidants

Contains vitamin C and polyphenols that combat oxidative stress and inflammation.

Supports digestion

Dietary fiber promotes regular bowel movements and a healthy gut microbiome.

Boosts immunity

Vitamins A and C support immune function and skin health.

Heart-friendly

Potassium helps regulate blood pressure and supports cardiovascular health.

Weight management

Low-calorie fruit with fiber increases satiety and helps maintain healthy weight.

Skin health

Antioxidants and vitamins in peaches help protect skin from oxidative damage and aging.

Ways to enjoy peaches

Fresh snack

Wash and enjoy raw peaches as a refreshing and sweet snack.

Peach smoothies

Blend peaches with yogurt or almond milk for a nutritious smoothie.

Baked peaches

Roast peaches with cinnamon for a healthy dessert.

Serving tips & cautions

  • Eat with peel to maximize fiber and antioxidants.
  • Choose ripe, fresh peaches for best nutrient content.
  • Watch portion if on blood sugar medication; peaches contain natural sugars.
