Top health benefits

Heart health

Monounsaturated fats help reduce LDL cholesterol and support cardiovascular health.

Bone health

Magnesium, phosphorus, and copper in cashews support strong bones and connective tissue.

Antioxidants

Contains phenolic compounds and vitamin E to help fight oxidative stress.

Weight management

Healthy fats and protein improve satiety and can help control appetite.

Blood sugar support

Low carbohydrate content and healthy fats help maintain steady blood sugar levels.

Brain health

Magnesium and healthy fats support cognitive function and nervous system health.

Ways to enjoy cashews

Snack on roasted cashews

Lightly roasted or raw cashews make a nutrient-dense snack.

Nut butter

Blend cashews into creamy cashew butter for spreads or smoothies.

Cooking & baking

Add cashews to stir-fries, curries, or baked goods for flavor and crunch.

Tips & cautions

  • Storage: Keep cashews in a cool, dry place or fridge to prevent rancidity.
  • Portion control: Cashews are calorie-dense; a small handful is recommended.
  • Allergies: Avoid if allergic to tree nuts.
  • Versatility: Use in savory and sweet dishes for flavor and nutrition.
