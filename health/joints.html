Degrees of Freedom in Human Joints

✋ Hand (27 DOF)

The hand has multiple DOFs across finger joints enabling grasp, pinch, rotation, and dexterity.

  • Finger stretches
  • Grip strengthening
  • Wrist circles

🦵 Knee (2 DOF)

Mainly hinge motion with slight internal/external rotation.

  • Squats
  • Hamstring stretches
  • Step-ups

🦶 Ankle (3 DOF)

Allows foot lift/drop and side tilts for balance.

  • Calf raises
  • Ankle circles
  • Balance drills

Daily Joint Health Routine (15–20 min)

  1. 5 min mobility warm-up
  2. Hand grip + finger extension (3 sets)
  3. Bodyweight squats (15 reps × 3)
  4. Single-leg balance (30 sec each)
  5. Stretch wrists, hamstrings, calves
