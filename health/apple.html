Top health benefits

Heart health

Apples contain soluble fiber that helps lower cholesterol and reduce heart disease risk.

Digestive support

Fiber-rich apples promote healthy gut microbiota and regular bowel movements.

Antioxidants

Flavonoids and vitamin C protect against oxidative stress and inflammation.

Weight management

Low-calorie, high-fiber apples increase satiety and help control appetite.

Immune support

Vitamin C and antioxidants support a healthy immune system.

Bone health

Some compounds in apples help improve bone density and strength.

Ways to enjoy apples

Fresh snack

Wash and eat raw apples as a crisp, refreshing snack.

Apple slices with nut butter

Pair apple slices with almond or peanut butter for added protein.

Baked apple dishes

Use apples in pies, crisps, or roasted with cinnamon for dessert.

Tips & cautions

  • Storage: Keep apples in the fridge for longer shelf life.
  • Variety: Choose different apple varieties for diverse nutrients.
  • Allergies: Some may have oral allergy syndrome to raw apples.
  • Preparation: Eat with peel to maximize fiber and antioxidants.
