Top health benefits

Heart health

Monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats may help reduce LDL cholesterol and promote cardiovascular health.

Weight management

Protein, fiber, and healthy fats increase satiety and may aid in controlling calorie intake.

Blood sugar control

Low glycemic index and fiber content help stabilize blood sugar levels when eaten with meals.

Rich in antioxidants

Lutein, zeaxanthin, and polyphenols help protect cells from oxidative stress.

Eye health

Lutein and zeaxanthin in pistachios help maintain healthy vision and reduce the risk of age-related macular degeneration.

Gut health

High fiber content supports healthy digestion and a balanced gut microbiome.

Easy ways to enjoy pistachios

Snack on them raw

A handful of raw or roasted pistachios makes a nutrient-dense snack.

Baking & desserts

Add chopped pistachios to cookies, cakes, or granola for crunch and flavor.

Salads & toppings

Sprinkle shelled pistachios on salads, yogurt, or oatmeal for added nutrients.

Tips & cautions

  • Storage: Keep in an airtight container in a cool place or fridge to prevent rancidity.
  • Portion control: Pistachios are calorie-dense; a small handful (~28g) is recommended.
  • Allergies: Avoid if allergic to tree nuts.
  • Versatility: Use in sweet or savory dishes for flavor and nutrition.
