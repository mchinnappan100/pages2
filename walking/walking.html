🚶‍♀️🏃 Walking & Running Facts

Explore interesting insights about your steps, distance, and health

🦶 Steps in a Mile

🚶 Walking: ~2,000–2,500 steps per mile
🏃 Running: ~1,000–1,700 steps per mile

Depends on stride length (average walking stride ~2.1–2.5 feet)

📏 2 Miles = How Many Steps?

Walking 2 miles takes about 4,000–5,000 steps

Running 2 miles = 2,000–3,400 steps

🔥 Calories Burned

On average:

  • Walking (3 mph): ~80–100 calories/mile
  • Running (6 mph): ~100–130 calories/mile

Varies by weight, speed, and metabolism

🎉 Fun Fact

10,000 steps is about 5 miles – a common daily step goal!

Reaching this daily can boost heart health, mood, and endurance.

⏱️ How Long Does 2 Miles Take?

🚶 Walking: ~30–40 minutes
🏃 Running: ~15–20 minutes

Depends on your pace and terrain

💡 Tip for Beginners

Start with 15–20 minutes a day. Track steps with your phone or smartwatch, and gradually increase your distance.
