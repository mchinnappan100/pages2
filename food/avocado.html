Top health benefits

Heart & cholesterol

High in monounsaturated fats (especially oleic acid), avocados help lower LDL (bad) cholesterol and may raise HDL (good) cholesterol â€” supporting heart health and reducing cardiovascular risk.

Rich in fiber

A typical avocado provides around 10 grams of fiber, helping appetite regulation, steady blood sugar, and a healthy gut microbiome.

Loaded with vitamins & minerals

Vitamins K, E, C, folate (B9), potassium and magnesium â€” avocados are nutrient-dense and support bone health, immunity, and cellular repair.

Anti-inflammatory & antioxidants

Contains lutein, zeaxanthin, and other antioxidants that protect cells from oxidative stress and help reduce inflammation.

Eye health

Lutein and zeaxanthin are linked to reduced risk of age-related macular degeneration and help protect the retina.

Weight & satiety

Healthy fats and fiber increase feelings of fullness, which can help control calorie intake when used as part of a balanced diet.

Easy ways to enjoy avocados

Classic: Avocado toast

Mashed avocado, squeeze of lemon, flaky salt, chili flakes on whole-grain toast. Add a poached egg for protein.

Creamy dressing / sauce

Blend avocado with yogurt or olive oil, lemon, garlic, and herbs â€” use as a salad dressing or dip.

Smoothie booster

Add 1/4 avocado to green smoothies for creaminess and healthy fats that slow sugar absorption.
