Tips & cautions

  • Ripeness: Give kiwis a gentle squeeze ‚Äî soft (but not mushy) means ripe. Store unripe at room temp; refrigerate ripe fruit.
  • Allergies: Some people have oral allergy syndrome with kiwi (itchy mouth/throat) ‚Äî avoid if you have severe reactions.
  • Enzyme sensitivity: Actinidin can tenderize proteins; avoid prolonged contact with milk-based gels or gelatin if texture matters.
  • Pairing: Vitamin C helps your body absorb iron from plant foods; add kiwi to iron-rich meals for better uptake.
