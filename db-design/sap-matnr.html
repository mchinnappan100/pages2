SAP Material Number (MATNR)

Understanding how SKU / Material Numbers behave in SAP systems

Can MATNR Be Null?

Short answer: No — MATNR (Material Number) cannot be null in SAP.

MATNR is the primary key for all material master records. It must always contain a value, cannot be empty, and must be unique across the system.

Why MATNR Cannot Be Null

  • It is the primary key for the material master.
  • Every material must be uniquely identifiable.
  • SAP enforces a NOT NULL constraint on MATNR.
  • Exists in all key material tables (MARA, MARC, MAKT, MBEW, etc.).

When MATNR Appears Null

Sometimes MATNR looks blank, but isn't truly null. Common reasons:

  • Leading zeros suppressed in SAP GUI displays.
  • Custom Z-tables may allow MATNR to be optional.
  • Document lines (POs, SOs) may not require a material (e.g., text or service items).
