Salesforce Steps Manager

A powerful tool for managing and editing steps in your Salesforce workflows. Streamline your development process with an intuitive interface for step selection and editing.

Key Features

Step Editor

Usage Instructions

Simply click on the desired step in the workflow overview to load it into the editor. Make your modifications and save to apply changes across your Salesforce environment.

Getting Started

  1. Install the Salesforce Steps Manager app via the Salesforce AppExchange or GitHub repository.
  2. Navigate to the Steps tab in your Salesforce org.
  3. Load your workflow and select a step to begin editing.
  4. Utilize the editor tools to customize and optimize your steps.
