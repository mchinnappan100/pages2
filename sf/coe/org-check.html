üîë Key Takeaways

  • Salesforce Optimizer is being retired, starting with newer orgs, and will no longer be the recommended tool for org analysis.
  • Org Check is a free Salesforce Labs solution designed to analyze org health, technical debt, and metadata complexity.
  • It helps admins identify issues like unused metadata, excessive permissions, complex role hierarchies, and configuration risks.
  • Org Check runs inside your Salesforce org and presents actionable insights through reports and dashboards.
  • Salesforce recommends installing and evaluating Org Check in a sandbox or developer org before production.
  • The tool is open source and community-driven, making it transparent and extensible.
