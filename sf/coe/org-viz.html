What is Org Viz?

Generate comprehensive visual diagrams of your Salesforce org metadata, relationships, and structure. The tool creates an interactive Org.svg file that automatically opens in your browser, allowing you to explore your org architecture at a glance.

Setup Guide

1

Install the Mohanc Salesforce Plugin

Install the Mohanc plugin to add the sf mohanc namespace to your Salesforce CLI. 

sf plugins:install sfdx-mohanc-plugins
2

Authenticate to Your Salesforce Org

Connect to your Salesforce org using web-based authentication. 

sf auth web login -r <instance_url>

Common Instance URLs:

login.salesforce.com
test.salesforce.com
mydomain.my.salesforce.com
3

Generate the Visualization

Run the visualization command using your authenticated username or alias. 

sf mohanc org viz -u <username>

This analyzes your org's metadata structure and generates an SVG diagram with all relationships.

4

Explore Your Org Structure

Once complete, the diagram automatically opens in your browser.

Interactive Viewing

Embed in docs, diagrams, or architecture reviews
