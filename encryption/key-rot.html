Key Rotation Strategies, Best Practices, and Implementation Guide

Technical White Paper

Version 1.0 | 2026

Executive Summary

Salesforce Platform Encryption provides robust data protection at rest using AES-256 encryption for sensitive data stored across Salesforce services. Key rotation is a critical security practice that limits the exposure window of encryption keys and maintains compliance with regulatory requirements.

This white paper provides a comprehensive guide to key rotation in Salesforce Platform Encryption, covering tenant secrets, data-in-use re-encryption strategies, risk mitigation, and operational best practices for organizations implementing enterprise-grade data security.

Key Takeaways:

  • • Regular key rotation reduces cryptographic exposure and meets compliance requirements
  • • Salesforce supports both manual and automated key rotation workflows
  • • Proper planning minimizes business disruption during rotation operations
  • • Understanding encryption scope is critical for successful implementation

Conclusion

Key rotation in Salesforce Platform Encryption is a critical security practice that balances protection, compliance, and operational efficiency. Organizations should view key rotation not as a burden but as an opportunity to validate their encryption architecture and demonstrate security maturity.
