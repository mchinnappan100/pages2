A Statement of Work (SOW) is a formal contract document that defines the scope, deliverables, timelines, and responsibilities of a project. It ensures that both the client and service provider share a clear understanding of what will be done, how it will be done, and when it will be completed.

ğŸ§© What a SOW Covers

1. Introduction & Purpose

Defines the background, goals, and high-level objectives of the project.

2. Scope of Work

Specifies the exact work to be performed and explicitly states exclusions.

3. Deliverables

Lists all tangible outputs such as reports, code, or documentation.

4. Schedule & Milestones

Provides project start and end dates with defined checkpoints.

5. Roles & Responsibilities

Defines who (client or vendor) owns each task or deliverable.

6. Acceptance Criteria

Explains how deliverables will be reviewed, tested, and approved.
