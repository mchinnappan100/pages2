Discover the mathematical elegance of Carnatic music through the 72 Melakarta scales. Each scale is a unique permutation of the 12 semitones, creating an exhaustive framework for melodic composition. About Indian Classical Music

Carnatic Music (South Indian)

Known for its mathematical precision, Carnatic music developed in the temples and courts of South India. It emphasizes structured composition and the 72 Melakarta system, providing a comprehensive framework for all possible ragas.

Hindustani Music (North Indian)

Emphasizing improvisation and emotional expression, Hindustani music evolved with influences from Persian and Islamic musical traditions. It values spontaneity within the structured framework of ragas.
