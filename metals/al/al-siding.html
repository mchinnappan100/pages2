♻️ Creative Uses for Old Aluminum Siding

Turn scrap into something beautiful and useful.

Don't Sell It for Scrap...

Old aluminum siding is lightweight, rust resistant, weatherproof, and can last for decades. Instead of getting only scrap value, transform it into functional structures, beautiful landscaping, or modern outdoor décor.

🌱 Sustainable 💰 Save Money 🔨 DIY Friendly ♻️ Eco Friendly

Top DIY Projects

🌱

Raised Garden Beds

Create durable raised beds that won't rot like wood. Perfect for vegetables, herbs, and flowers.

  • ✔ Rust resistant
  • ✔ Modern appearance
  • ✔ Long lasting
🛡️

Privacy Screens

Hide garbage bins, HVAC units, patios or create decorative outdoor walls.

  • ✔ Backyard divider
  • ✔ Decorative fencing
  • ✔ Wind protection
🏠

Garden Shed

Reuse enough siding from one house to build an entire storage shed.

  • ✔ Weatherproof
  • ✔ Low maintenance
  • ✔ Saves thousands
🐔

Chicken Coop

Excellent material for poultry shelters, rabbit hutches and livestock housing.

🎨

Metal Artwork

Turn old siding into sculptures, wall art, flowers and decorative outdoor pieces.

🚜

Workshop Interior

Use as interior walls and ceilings for garages, barns and workshops.

🪵

Firewood Storage

Build a covered wood storage area that keeps logs dry all year.

🌿

Greenhouse

Use aluminum for lower walls while using clear polycarbonate for the roof.

🚲

Bike Shelter

Protect bicycles, lawn equipment and outdoor tools from rain and snow.

Estimated Value

A complete home's aluminum siding can often provide enough material for projects worth several thousand dollars—far exceeding its scrap value.

🛠️ Safety Tips

  • 🧤 Wear cut-resistant gloves.
  • 🥽 Use eye protection when cutting.
  • 🧼 Wash panels before reuse.
  • 🏡 Test painted surfaces for lead if very old.
  • 📐 Reinforce structural projects with lumber or steel framing.

Pro Tip

Aluminum siding is incredibly durable. When combined with cedar or powder-coated steel, it can produce modern-looking outdoor furniture, planters, fences and sheds that last for decades.
