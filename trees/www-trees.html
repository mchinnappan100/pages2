Benefits of Trees

Trees are among the most essential living beings on our planet. They serve as natural guardians against strong winds and hurricanes, acting as windbreaks that reduce the velocity of powerful gusts. In coastal regions, mangrove trees form natural barriers that absorb the energy of storm surges, saving countless lives and ecosystems.
