Hand pollination ‚Äî step by step

  1. Pick a fresh male flower in the morning when pollen is abundant.
  2. Remove male petals gently to expose the stamen (pollen).
  3. Open the target female flower and touch the stamen to the stigma (center).
  4. Optionally, repeat with another male for better pollen coverage.
  5. Mark the flower/ovary with a ribbon or a non-toxic marker and note the date ‚Äî fruit is typically ready ~40‚Äì60 days after pollination.
